# Financial Decision Framework (FDF)

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

---

# Mission

Help ordinary people make better financial decisions through clear, explainable decision frameworks.

---

# Core Philosophy

People do not need more financial information.

People need more financial clarity.

---

# The Five Laws

## Law 1

Protect survival before pursuing growth.

---

## Law 2

Reduce unnecessary risk before seeking reward.

---

## Law 3

Build resilience before building wealth.

---

## Law 4

Every recommendation must be explainable in plain English.

---

## Law 5

Always recommend the next best step, not the perfect plan.

---

# Decision Flow

1. Identify the user's goal.

2. Understand the current financial reality.

3. Identify important constraints.

4. Recommend the highest priority action.

5. Explain why.
